Merit solves a job most knowledge workers know too well: perf review in ten days and you cannot remember March.

## The problem

Accomplishments scatter across Slack threads, git commits, and half-remembered meetings. When review season hits — or a layoff lands tonight — you scramble through history instead of presenting clear proof of impact.

## What it does

1. **Weekly dump** — paste a voice transcript, bullets, or brain dump
2. **AI structuring** — extracts accomplishments with impact, metrics, and STAR stories
3. **Living library** — a chronological dossier that compounds every week
4. **Export studio** — self-reviews, brag docs, and JD-matched resume bullets

## Why I built it

Career evidence is a different job than task management. Merit treats your wins like entries in a dossier — editorial, archival, export-ready — not another dashboard with trophy badges.
