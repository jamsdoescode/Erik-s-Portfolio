Relay is a context-reload tool for people who juggle multiple projects and lose twenty minutes every time they switch back.

## The problem

Context switching costs roughly twenty minutes per jump. You open a project you have not touched in three days and spend that time re-reading Slack, Notion, and git history just to remember where you were.

## What it does

1. **Projects** — one context per codebase, client, or initiative
2. **Resume session** — start the clock when you begin working
3. **Stop and relay** — log what you did, what is next, and blockers when you switch away
4. **Relay brief** — AI synthesizes your last sessions into a thirty-second context reload

## Why I built it

The job is not remembering everything. It is reloading fast enough to start doing the work again.
