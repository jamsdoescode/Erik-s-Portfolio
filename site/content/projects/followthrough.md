FollowThrough turns unstructured text into an actionable follow-up system for professionals who make dozens of implicit promises every week.

## The problem

"I'll send that deck by Friday." "Let me intro you to Sarah." "Waiting on legal." These live buried in notes, threads, and chat logs. Generic todo apps require manual entry. Missing a follow-up costs reputation.

## What it does

1. **Capture** — paste any text; AI extracts who owes what, to whom, and by when
2. **Prioritize** — overdue, due today, upcoming, and waiting-on buckets
3. **Digest** — daily morning briefing of what needs attention
4. **Ask** — natural language queries about your commitments
5. **Draft** — one-click follow-up message generation

## Why I built it

Commitments are implicit in conversation. FollowThrough makes them explicit without adding friction to how you already work.
